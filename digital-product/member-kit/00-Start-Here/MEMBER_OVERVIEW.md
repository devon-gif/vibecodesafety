# Member Overview

VibeCode Safety Membership gives you a repeatable system for reviewing AI-generated code.

## Membership includes

- Heavy-duty repo audit prompt.
- Risk map and audit report templates.
- Tool-specific auditor-agent instructions.
- Daily AI change review prompts.
- Pre-commit, pre-push, and pre-deploy guardrails.
- Monthly safety drops.
- Support and cancellation instructions.

## Heavy-duty repo audit

Run this when you are starting a project, preparing to launch, inheriting a repo, or making a risky change. The audit should identify affected files, high-risk flows, missing checks, commands to run, and a PASS / WARNING / BLOCKED verdict.

## Daily guardrails

Use the daily guardrails after every AI change. The goal is to catch risk while the change is still fresh and small.

## Monthly drops

Monthly drops are focused safety packs. They do not replace the full repo audit. They help you review one risk area deeply, such as Stripe checkout, Supabase RLS, or auth sessions.

## Limits

This membership is self-serve. It does not include manual human audits at $6.99/month. It does not guarantee security or replace professional review.
