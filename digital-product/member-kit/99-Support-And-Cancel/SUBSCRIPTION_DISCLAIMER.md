# Subscription Disclaimer

Read before using VibeCode Safety.

---

## What the subscription includes

The VibeCode Safety membership includes:
- The current member kit (digital files, prompts, and checklists)
- The VibeCode Auditor Agent instructions for AI tools
- The heavy-duty repo audit workflow
- Daily build guardrail prompts
- Monthly safety drops

---

## What the subscription does NOT include

- Manual human repo audits performed by Devon or any other person
- Guaranteed security for your application
- Guaranteed bug-free or production-ready software
- Legal, compliance, or regulatory advice
- A replacement for professional engineering, QA, or a security audit
- Any form of automated repo scanning or AI with access to your codebase
  (the AI access is provided by your own tools — Claude, Cursor, Codex, etc.)

---

## How the audit workflow works

The heavy-duty repo audit is an AI-assisted workflow. You run it yourself,
using your own AI coding tool, with your own repo context. The workflow
provides structured prompts and checklists to guide the review. The AI does
the analysis. The results are only as good as the context you provide.

No one at VibeCode Safety receives or accesses your code. The audit is
entirely local to your tools.

---

## Risk and limitation

Using VibeCode Safety does not mean your application is secure, safe to ship,
or free of bugs. It means you ran a structured review process. That reduces
risk. It does not eliminate it.

For applications handling real user data, payments, or sensitive information,
consider a professional security review in addition to these workflows.
