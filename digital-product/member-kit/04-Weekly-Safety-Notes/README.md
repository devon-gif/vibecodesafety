# Weekly Safety Notes

Weekly Safety Notes are small, practical updates for members.

Each note focuses on one risk AI-assisted builders commonly miss. Use them when the topic matches the part of your app you are changing.

## How to use

1. Pick the note that matches your current work.
2. Read the risk summary.
3. Paste the audit prompt into your AI coding tool with the relevant files or diff.
4. Run the quick checklist before commit, push, or deploy.
5. Treat BLOCKED as a stop sign.

Weekly notes are not a guarantee of safety. They help you build a stronger review habit.
