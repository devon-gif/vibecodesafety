# READ THIS FIRST

Welcome. You just bought VibeCode Safety Kit. This page is the shortest
honest answer to *"what do I do now?"*.

---

## The kit, in one sentence

> Copy 3 files into your repo. Paste 1 prompt after every AI coding
> change. Don&rsquo;t ship until the review passes.

---

## What to do in the next 5 minutes

1. Open `5_MINUTE_SETUP.md` in this folder.
2. Follow it step by step.
3. When you finish, come back and skim `WHICH_FILE_DO_I_USE.md` so you
   know what each file is for.

That&rsquo;s it. You can ignore the rest of the kit until you feel

---

## What the kit actually is

A small folder of plain markdown files:

- **Rules** that tell your AI coding assistant how to behave in your repo.
- **Prompts** you paste into your AI tool before and after every change.
- **Checklists** you run before commit, push, and deploy.
- **Risk maps** that show what usually breaks (so you can avoid it).

It is **not** a course. It is **not** a SaaS subscription. It is **not**
a security audit. It is **not** a replacement for a senior developer.

It is a habit, in markdown form.

---

## What it expects from you

- You can copy a file from one folder to another.
- You can paste a prompt into your AI tool.
- You can read a short report and follow simple steps.

You do not need to be a senior engineer. If you are using Cursor,
Claude, Codex, Windsurf, Copilot, Lovable, Bolt, Replit, or any similar
tool to build apps, you can use this kit.

## Honest disclaimer

This kit reduces risk. It does not guarantee bug-free or secure
software. Anything storing money, health data, or PII at scale should
also get a real engineering and security review.

Use the kit to slow down for the right five minutes before you ship.
